## Relevance for WG3 (Education)

Aim 3 maps directly to WG3's work: "Identify critical educational and competency gaps in AI for emergency care providers." The grant specifically calls for:
- Comprehensive educational needs assessment
- Foundational knowledge gaps and emerging competencies
- Future educational initiatives, curricula development, and CPE programs
- AI literacy, human-computer interaction, and data-informed clinical decision-making
