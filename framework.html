Discussion prompts for the WG3 meeting

  1. Do the existing four themes still cover the territory once stress-tested against 14 concrete EM scenarios? Where do they strain?
  2. Which of the candidate new themes deserve first-class status vs. subtheme status vs. being folded into existing themes?
  3. Which scenarios are most urgent to teach now, vs. which can wait for v2 of the framework?
  4. Does "communicating about AI" need to split into patient-facing and team-facing competencies?
  5. What's missing entirely? Ambient scribing, patient-brought-AI, and metacognition came up on the kickoff call but aren't on Carl's 14.
  6. Does the academic / community persona dimension change the competency weights for any scenario?
  7. Where does the framework overlap with WG4 (Training) — and what's the explicit hand-off?
